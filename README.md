# 🧘‍♀️ VR Therapy Hub

**VR Therapy Hub** is a virtual reality platform that provides calming environments, interactive exercises, and guided sessions to support mental wellness and therapy in an immersive way.

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🕶️ **Immersive VR Environments** – Experience calm and peaceful virtual worlds designed for relaxation and therapy.  
- 💬 **Guided Wellness Sessions** – Interactive exercises and mindfulness sessions for stress relief and focus.  
- 🎨 **Custom Scenes** – Different environments such as air, sky, and outdoor therapy sessions (`air.html`, `sky.html`, `ot.html`).  
- 🧠 **Mental Wellness Support** – Focused on improving emotional and psychological well-being through virtual immersion.
